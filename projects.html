Apache Guacamole

Apache Guacamole is a clientless remote desktop gateway. It supports standard protocols like VNC, RDP, and SSH. It is called clientless because no plugins or client software are required. Thanks to HTML5, once Guacamole is installed on a server, all you need to access your desktops is a web browser.

Presence-Detector

Have you ever needed someone's help, but you weren't sure if they are on a meeting, in a phone call or just listening to some music? Well, i and many other of my Colleagues at work ask ourselves this exact question. So i took it upon myself to stop this madness. Rather my Supervisor told to. With his help, i built a little LED-Light, which sits on top of you monitor and shines Green if you are available and red if you are in a meeting or on the phone.
